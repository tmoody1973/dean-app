## Video direction

Palette system: warm cream ground, near-black headlines, muted gray support copy, and cobalt as the single visible accent. Screenshots are the proof layer; overlays are short labels, rails, pills, and progress marks only.

Motion grammar: every frame uses smooth long-tail movement, VO-paced reveals, and restrained cobalt emphasis. No bouncy entrances, no generic AI gradients, no invented product UI, no front-loaded slide dumps, and no screensaver drifting. Deliberate held reads: Frames 5 and 8.

Caption keep-out: all load-bearing content stays above the bottom 17% of the 1920x1080 canvas.
