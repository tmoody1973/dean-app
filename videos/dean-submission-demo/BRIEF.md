## Intent

A concise product showcase for the Dean Build Week submission. It should lead
with the difference between a generic chat tutor and Dean's visible curriculum
compiler, then prove it with real production screens: the three curated tracks,
the calibration questions, the seven-file curriculum birth, and the first
interactive lesson. The tone is clear, confident, and evidence-led rather than
hype-driven.
